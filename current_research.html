Current research

I am working on my PhD qualitative research on catalan archaeology researchers' habits and perspectives about open data. Soon I'll be presenting the preliminary results of my research in CAA.

At the moment I'm in York, working with the Archaeology Data Service to learn from their more than 25 years experience archiving archaeological data.

At the same time, I am the co-director of the excavations in the site Vilavella del Castellet, and I collaborate with the Laboratori d'Arqueologia Medieval de la Universitat de Barcelona (LAMUB).

I'm also coordinating the weekly publication Blok de BiD
