Current research

I am working on my PhD qualitative research on catalan archaeology researchers' habits and perspectives about open data. Soon I'll be presenting the methodology and the preliminary results of my research in DAB23 (see poster here) and CAA.

At the moment I'm in York (United Kingdom), working with the Archaeology Data Service to learn from their more than 25 years experience archiving archaeological data.

At the same time, I am the co-director of the excavations in the site Vilavella del Castellet, within the project Muntanya Viva (CLT009/18/00041), and I collaborate with the Laboratori d'Arqueologia Medieval de la Universitat de Barcelona (LAMUB).
